Oorja Group and its subsidiaries are raising awareness and advising caution to the public of known deception acts related to recruitment process, which have come to our attention recently, where prospective job applicants were contacted by individuals claiming to be a representative of mining companies, and requesting a sum of money for employment opportunities with the promise to be reimbursed. Job applicants are encouraged to follow a formal recruitment program directly carried out by any Company. Oorja Group and its subsidiaries do not collect any fee whatsoever for our recruitment process.

Job Description:
  • Presenting daily unit breakdown monitoring reports in the form of daily unit breakdown reports.
  • Reviewing the service schedule (planning service) periodically for all units and ensuring that part service for all units is available in the warehouse/logistics.
  • Create and monitor Daily Physical Availability (Daily PA) reports in the form of daily, weekly, monthly and yearly reports.
  • Planning the Budget Requirements Plan for tools, equipment, tires, oil & lubricants, parts service, spare parts and general goods materials (BBU) workshops every month and yearly.
  • Make data mapping of the needs of spare parts, and tools that will be used by mechanics to complete the target unit breakdown.

  • Have good Proficiency in using Microsoft Office and analyzing data
  • Able to make reports professionally
  • Have a good communication and leadership skill

Job Description:
  • Create a mine plan map that describes the crest toe, ramp, sump, elevation and others
  • Make daily mine design/planning which includes: work location (excavation location), work area boundary (boundary pit), mining sequence, total OB and Coal production plan, material stockpiling location and capacity (Waste Dump), equipment allocation (fleet allocation), road (ramp), mine drainage, and estimation of OB discharge distance.
  • Ensure monthly plans are in place to create daily and weekly mining sequences.
  • Evaluate with a high level of complexity the total volume of OB material and coal that has been mined in the pit periodically.
  • Conduct daily field visits to control the mining implementation so that it is in accordance with the plans that have been made and provide recommendations for corrective actions with a high level of complexity if needed.

Make a plan review and evaluation of the actual, which includes stripping limits, boundaries and elevations, as well as factors that affect the deviation between the plan and actual. Prepare and publish the Minutes of Monthly Volume Calculation Results in the form of draft Minutes of Invoicing (BAI).
